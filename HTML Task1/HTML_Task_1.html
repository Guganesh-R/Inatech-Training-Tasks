Recipe: Cheese Sandwich

This recipe will show you how to make a delicious food item that's sure to impress your friends and family. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create a delicious dish that's sure to be a hit.

Ingredients:

  1. Cheese
  2. Whole Wheat Bread
  3. Butter
  4. Chilli Flakes
  5. Crushed Black Pepper

Step by Step:

  1. Slice the bread into peices
  2. Apply Butter to both the sides of the bread slice
  3. Spread the cheese in the bread slice
  4. Sprinkle some chilli flakes and crushed black pepper
  5. Now Toast the slice in a pan with som butter
  6. Serve Hot
