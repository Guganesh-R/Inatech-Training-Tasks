Job Application Instruction

Instructions:

  1. Fill out your full name in the "Full Name" field.
  2. Enter your email address in the "Email" field.
  3. Enter your phone number in the "Phone Number" field.
  4. Type your address in the "Address" field.
  5. Upload your resume in the "Upload Resume" field.
  6. Click the "Submit" button to submit your job application.
Note: All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.
